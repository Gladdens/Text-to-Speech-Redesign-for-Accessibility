I made a sweet and creamy chocolate mousse cake~

♥For more detailed recipes and explanations, please click the More button below♥

You can fully feel the rich chocolate taste and the gelatin. It's made without gelatin, so it's soft and not sticky, so it melts in your mouth when you eat it~^^

Since there's no gelatin, it can easily crumble, so you have to be careful when taking it out of the mold.

It's good because it contains a lot of milk, so it's savory and can be made delicious with simple ingredients~~

Once you take a bite, it melts your mood and is perfect as a sweet dessert~*^^*

--------------------------------------------------------------

▶Mold size : 15.5cm x 7.8cm x 6.5cm

▶Ingredients
100g Warm whipping cream
20g Cocoa powder
550g Milk
60g Sugar
27g Corn starch
100g Dark chocolate

▶Recipe
1. Sift cocoa powder into warm whipped cream and mix.

2. Mix milk, sugar, and cornstarch (1) in a bowl, then put in a pot and boil over low heat while stirring. Then add dark chocolate and boil until slightly thickened.

3. Filter through a sieve, place in a mold, cool to room temperature, and harden in the refrigerator for more than 6 hours.
